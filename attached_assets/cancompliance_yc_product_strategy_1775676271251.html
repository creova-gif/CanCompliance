3 · AI use
Claude-powered compliance copilot
The control mapper is rule-based. That's a rules engine, not AI. YC in 2025 expects AI that reasons, not AI that pattern-matches. Two AI features needed.
NEW:"Ask CanCompliance" — Claude Sonnet answers any Canadian compliance question in plain English, with statute citations baked into every response
NEW:Website scanner — paste a URL, Claude reads the page and outputs: CASL violations, missing privacy policy clauses, greenwashing claims, missing French
NEW:Policy generator — "Generate my CASL-compliant privacy policy for a Quebec e-commerce business" → produces a real, cited, downloadable PDF
NEW:Plain-language explanations on every FAIL — Claude converts statutory language to "here's what this means and here's exactly how to fix it"

4 · Day 1 retention
Daily compliance triggers
The CASL ledger creates switching costs. But there's no daily reason to open the app. Need a mechanic that makes users return every day — like Duolingo's streak.
NEW:Compliance streak counter — "You've checked compliance 7 days in a row. Your score: 84/100." Streak breaks = score warning badge
NEW:Daily regulatory digest — "Today's update: Ontario minimum wage increases July 1. Your payroll module needs updating. [Fix now +3 pts]"
NEW:CASL expiry alerts — "3 implied consents expire in 7 days. Re-consent campaign needed." — shows up on dashboard every morning
NEW:Weekly compliance report card — emailed every Monday: score change, deadlines approaching, 1 action to improve score this week

5 · Growth loop
Viral sharing + referral mechanics
Currently zero viral coefficient. The growth loop must be built into the product itself. Compliance is inherently social — companies share certificates, lawyers refer clients, agencies share with their clients.
NEW:Shareable compliance certificate — one-click public URL: "ACME Inc. · Compliance score: 87/100 · CASL · PIPEDA · Ontario ESA · Verified by CanCompliance"
NEW:Agency client portal — agencies invite their clients: "Here's your compliance dashboard, managed by [Agency Name]" → agency pays, client uses → agency upsells compliance reviews
NEW:Referral program — "Refer a Canadian business, get 1 month free. They get 1 month free." Each referral tracked in dashboard
NEW:Compliance badge widget — embeddable trust badge for websites: "Privacy compliant · CASL certified · Powered by CanCompliance" — every embed is a backlink + impression
