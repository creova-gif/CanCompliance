CANCompliance powers modern compliance with intelligence, scale, and speed.

Driven by 2,000+ automated tests running continuously, and connected through a growing ecosystem of 400+ integrations, CANCompliance gives organizations real-time visibility into their compliance posture. The platform automatically gathers audit-ready evidence, centralizes collaboration across teams, and accelerates remediation with AI-generated, environment-specific code recommendations tailored to your infrastructure.

From the moment you log in, CANCompliance equips you with the technology, workflows, and expert-backed guidance needed to move from zero to audit-ready efficiently. A dynamic, in-app compliance roadmap walks you through every stage — from initial setup to passing audits — ensuring nothing is missed.

🔧 Core Capabilities

Policy Management
Create, customize, and manage policies effortlessly using a comprehensive library of framework-aligned templates. A guided policy builder simplifies editing, while automated tracking ensures employees review and acknowledge required policies in real time.

Workforce Compliance Management
Keep your team aligned with compliance standards through integrated security training modules, optional background verification workflows, and fully configurable onboarding and offboarding processes.

Control Mapping & Continuous Monitoring
Adopt pre-configured controls or introduce your own. CANCompliance AI intelligently maps controls to its expanding library of automated tests, enabling continuous validation and real-time compliance tracking across multiple frameworks.

Audit & Issue Management
Handle the entire audit lifecycle within a single platform. Prepare documentation, track findings, resolve issues efficiently, and collaborate directly with auditors — reducing friction and increasing audit success rates.

CANCompliance goes beyond checklists — it enables continuous, real-time compliance, empowering organizations to proactively manage risk, maintain trust, and scale with confidence.
