The 10 AI systems built
System 1 — AI Risk Scoring Engine. Weighted compliance risk model using real enforcement trend data. Each module has a base weight, enforcement citation (with real 2025-2026 case data), and trend direction (up/stable/down). FINTRAC gets 35 base weight + 25% trend multiplier = highest risk. The score now reflects actual enforcement patterns, not just pass/fail counts.
System 2 — Predictive Compliance Alert Engine. Five trigger-based alerts that fire based on real conditions: Bill C-12 in force (date check), CRTC enforcement surge (checks > 0 with fails), Quebec CAI joint audits (province = QC), Ontario AI hiring law (year = 2026), CASL expiry (checks ledger). Each alert injects a banner into the relevant panel automatically — you don't go looking for it, it comes to you.
System 3 — Smart Audit Trail v2. After every check, analyzes patterns: repeat failures in the same module, high-risk modules with no audit records. Surfaces insights below the audit table — "Pattern: 2 repeat FINTRAC failures — systematic issue detected, not a one-off."
System 4 — Compliance Copilot v2. Upgraded system prompt with April 8, 2026 enforcement context baked in — Bill C-12 penalties, CRTC 4 fines in Jan-Feb 2026, CPPA incoming, BC WorkSafeBC first enforcement actions. Context-aware: pulls your current province, compliance score, and recent failures and appends them to every question. Statute citations now render as styled monospace pills.
System 5 — Document Scanner v2. Specialized system prompts per document type (privacy policy gets PIPEDA + Law 25 + CPPA focus; employment contract gets ESA + Human Rights Code; terms of service gets CASL + CPLA + Competition Act). DoXpert-inspired: each document type gets a targeted, specialized analysis rather than generic multi-law scanning. 6,000 character input. Violations render with left border, fix in green, penalty in red monospace.
System 6 — Policy Generator. Five policies generated by Claude Sonnet 4: CASL consent capture form, PIPEDA + Law 25 privacy policy, CASL unsubscribe mechanism, Ontario electronic monitoring policy (ESA s.21.1.1, 25+ employees), BC psychological harassment prevention policy (mandatory Sept 2025). Each includes province, statute citations, and "Not legal advice — lawyer review required" disclaimer.
System 7 — FINTRAC Bill C-12 Patch. Automatically injects a live "IN FORCE" banner into the FINTRAC panel with the real March 26, 2026 effective date, the 40× penalty increase, and the new "effectiveness" violation standard. Rate table updated with $20M or 3% global revenue penalty.
System 8 — AI Remediation Planner. Button in the score panel: "Generate my remediation plan." Claude analyzes your actual failures and flags, your province, and generates a prioritized week-by-week 30-day remediation plan with specific statute citations and concrete actions.
System 9 — CASL Consent Health Analyzer. Analyzes the entire ledger: express vs. implied ratios, expiry risk, missing IP fields, missing consent text, unsubscribed rate. Generates a 0–100 consent health score and Claude-generated analysis of gaps with CASL-specific recommendations.
System 10 — Live Enforcement Ticker. Sticky banner below the topbar showing 10 real 2025-2026 enforcement actions scrolling continuously: the $176.9M FINTRAC penalty, the $1.1M CASL fine, Bill C-12 in force, WorkSafeBC first orders, Quebec joint audits. Every session opens with live enforcement context.
