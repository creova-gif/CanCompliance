Tanzania Finance Act 2025 — Live compliance checks
No compliance SaaS in the world covers both Canada and Tanzania. This is the global moat. WVAT · DST · TRA EFD · PDPA · EPOCA e-waste — all live.
TZ-A · Finance Act 2025
WVAT — Withholding VAT
3% for registered VAT taxpayers, 6% for non-registered. TRA interfacing mandatory — non-compliance: TZS 10M individual / TZS 60M entity. In force July 2025.
TZ-B · Finance Act 2025
DST — Digital Services Tax
2% on digital services revenue. Applies to non-resident providers supplying digital services in Tanzania. TRA registration required if services exceed TZS 14.7M threshold.
TZ-C · TRA EFD
EFD / VFD system interfacing
Electronic Fiscal Device mandatory for all VAT-registered businesses. Real-time TRA interfacing since 2023. Imprisonment risk for non-compliance. GoPay Tanzania integration path available.
TZ-D · PDPA 2022
Personal Data Protection Act
Tanzania's data protection law — consent requirements, data subject rights, cross-border transfer restrictions. Enforced by TCRA. Penalty: TZS 5M individual, TZS 15M entity.
TZ-E · EPOCA
E-waste (EPOCA)
Electronic and Electrical Equipment (EEE) producer responsibility. Importers and manufacturers of electronics must register with NEMC. Extended producer responsibility applies from 2024.
